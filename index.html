Frequently Asked Questions

Are the tools free to use?

Yes! All tools are completely free and run in your browser. We don’t store or upload your images.

Will my image quality be reduced?

No. Compression reduces file size but keeps the original resolution and visible quality intact.

Is Image Cut secure?

Absolutely. All image processing is done locally in your browser — nothing is ever sent to our servers.
