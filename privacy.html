Privacy Policy

At Image Cut, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Our tools are designed to process all actions directly in your browser — meaning your files are never uploaded, stored, or shared with any server.

1. Local Processing

All compression, conversion, and PDF generation take place entirely within your device’s browser using client-side code. None of your images or data leave your computer. Image Cut does not collect, store, or transmit your files.

2. Analytics and Cookies

We use basic analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how users interact with our site. These analytics collect non-personal data, such as page views and session duration. You can control cookie settings through your browser at any time.

3. Advertising

Image Cut may display Google-served ads in the future. These ads may use cookies to personalize content and measure performance. You can learn more about how Google uses your information by visiting Google’s Advertising Policy.

4. Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to external pages or tools. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any external services they visit.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our tools, features, or regulations. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out at contact@image-cut.com.
